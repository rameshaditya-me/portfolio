About

Hi! I’m Aditya Ramesh. I work as a Research Scientist II at Fujitsu Research, India.

I build learning systems with an emphasis on turning learning algorithms into working decision-making systems that operate reliably under uncertainty in complex environments. I emphasize reliability ; safety and adversarial robustness, effective behavior in sparse-reward settings, and stability over extended operation in partially observable or changing environments ; and I typically carry projects end-to-end: identifying a research gap, formulating and publishing a method, and implementing it in a working proof-of-concept system.

You can find publications and technical details on my research page and Google Scholar. I also write longer technical notes and project writeups here.
