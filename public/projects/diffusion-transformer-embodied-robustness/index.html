Diffusion Transformer with LoRA and Action-Consistency for Robust Embodied Control

Improved perception robustness for embodied decision-making by fine-tuning a Diffusion Transformer with LoRA and an action-consistency loss; increased resilience to RGB perturbations (illumination, blur, occlusion) and more stable control under uncertain or degraded visual inputs.
