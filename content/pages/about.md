      Hi — people from the Internet. I am **Aditya Ramesh**. I am a Research Scientist II at [Fujitsu Research, India](https://global.fujitsu/en-apac/local/technology/research), working on reinforcement learning and reliable autonomous agents.

  - label: "02 / Research"
    body: |
      I build learning systems with an emphasis on turning algorithms into working decision-making systems that operate reliably under uncertainty — spanning safety and adversarial robustness, sparse-reward RL, and stable long-horizon control.

      * **Long-horizon robotics**: training-free memory-conditioned action generation for frozen VLAs ([ICML SCALE 2026](project.html?src=content/papers/beyond-the-moment-vla-memory.md)).
      * **Reliable RL & sparse rewards**: graph-theoretic intrinsic rewards and related methods for hard exploration ([ICLR 2026](project.html?src=content/papers/graph-theoretic-intrinsic-reward-rl.md)).
      * **LLM safety**: efficient jailbreak attack sequences and evaluation ([ICLR 2025](project.html?src=content/papers/efficient-jailbreak-attack-sequences.md)).
      * **Signal processing**: fixed-budget kernel LMS with random Fourier features ([RadarConf 2020](project.html?src=content/papers/fixed-budget-kernel-lms.md)).
